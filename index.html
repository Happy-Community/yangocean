Start with Small Daily Habits

You do not need major changes to improve your life. Small consistent actions are enough.

  • Take a few minutes each morning to breathe calmly
  • Focus on one task at a time
  • Take short breaks from screens every hour
  • Spend time with family or loved ones regularly
💡 Consistency matters more than perfection. Start small and build gradually.
