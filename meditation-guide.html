Simple Meditation Guide (Calm Breathing Practice)

This guide helps beginners practice calm breathing meditation by focusing on natural awareness of the breath. It focuses on calming the mind through awareness of breathing.

Step 1: Find a Quiet Place

Sit in a comfortable and stable position. Keep your back straight but relaxed.

Step 2: Relax Your Body

Gently relax your body from head to toe and allow natural breathing.

Step 3: Focus on the Breath

Observe the breath at one steady point without following it.

Step 4: Stay with One Object

Keep awareness on a single point of breathing consistently.

Step 5: Handle Distractions

When thoughts arise, gently return attention to the breath.

Step 6: Build Continuity

With practice, the mind becomes calmer and more stable.

Conclusion

Meditation improves clarity and calmness through daily practice.
