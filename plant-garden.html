Plant a Tree & Grow a Home Garden 🌱

Why It Matters

Planting trees and growing plants improves air quality, reduces stress, and creates a more peaceful environment at home.

How to Plant a Tree

Choose a sunny location
Dig a hole twice the root size
Place tree gently in soil
Water thoroughly after planting
Add mulch to retain moisture
Water regularly in early growth stage

Start a Simple Home Garden

  • Start with easy plants like mint, basil, or spinach
  • Use pots if you have limited space
  • Ensure daily sunlight (4–6 hours)
  • Water lightly but consistently
  • Use natural compost if available

Beginner Tips

  • Start small (even 1 plant is enough)
  • Avoid overwatering
  • Observe plant growth daily
  • Be patient — growth takes time
Gardening helps both nature and your mental well-being.
