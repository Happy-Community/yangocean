Plant a Tree & Grow a Home Garden 🌱

Simple steps to reconnect with nature and create a greener world

Why it matters

Planting trees and growing your own garden helps improve air quality, reduce stress, support biodiversity, and bring more calmness into daily life.

🌳 How to plant a tree (simple guide)

1. Choose a suitable location with sunlight
2. Dig a hole twice the width of the root ball
3. Place the tree gently and fill soil back
4. Water thoroughly after planting
5. Add mulch to keep moisture
6. Water regularly in early weeks

🏡 Start a simple home garden

  • Start with easy plants: chili, basil, mint, spinach
  • Use pots if you have no land or balcony
  • Ensure 4–6 hours of sunlight daily
  • Water lightly but consistently
  • Use natural compost if possible

🌿 Beginner tips

  • Start small — even 1 pot is enough
  • Don’t overwater plants
  • Observe daily changes (this builds mindfulness too)
  • Be patient — plants grow slowly

Gardening is not only about plants — it is also about calmness, patience, and awareness.

🌏 Small action, big impact

Even planting one tree or growing one herb plant contributes to a healthier planet. Start simple, stay consistent.
