Plant a Tree & Grow a Home Garden 🌱

Planting trees and growing plants is one of the simplest ways to improve well-being and contribute to a healthier environment.

Why It Matters

Gardening improves air quality, reduces stress, and creates a sense of calm and connection with nature.

How to Plant a Tree

Choose a sunny location
Dig a hole twice root size
Place tree carefully
Fill soil and press gently
Water thoroughly
Add mulch for moisture

Start a Home Garden

  • Start with easy plants like mint, basil, chili
  • Use pots if space is limited
  • Ensure daily sunlight exposure
  • Water consistently but not excessively

Beginner Tips

  • Start small and grow gradually
  • Observe plant growth daily
  • Be patient with results

Conclusion

Even one plant can make a difference. Start simple and stay consistent.
