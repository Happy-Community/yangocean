Simple Stress Management Tips

Stress is part of life, but it can be managed through simple daily habits.

1. Deep Breathing

Slow breathing helps calm the nervous system.

2. Stay Active

Walking or light exercise helps release tension.

3. Healthy Routine

Good sleep and regular meals support mental balance.

4. Reduce Overthinking

Focus only on what you can control.

5. Relax Daily

Take time for music, reading, or nature.

Conclusion

Small habits create long-term calmness.
